Nedbank Namibia module
======================

What this institution is
------------------------

Nedbank Namibia is a commercial bank operating in Namibia as part of the wider
Nedbank Group. It offers retail, business, and corporate banking services,
including deposit accounts, lending products, and payment services.

Why it matters in the financial system
--------------------------------------

Nedbank Namibia is one of the key providers of:

- Credit intermediation for households and businesses
- Transaction accounts and payment services
- Channels for savings, investments, and risk management products
