Namclear module
===============

What this institution is
------------------------

Namclear is a key payment system operator in Namibia. It provides clearing and
settlement services for various payment instruments, enabling interbank
transfers and other forms of electronic payments between participating
institutions.

Why it matters in the financial system
--------------------------------------

Namclear is part of the core financial market infrastructure in Namibia:

- It enables reliable, timely clearing and settlement of payments.
- It connects banks and other participants through common rails.
- It influences the speed, cost, and risk profile of retail and wholesale
  payment flows.
