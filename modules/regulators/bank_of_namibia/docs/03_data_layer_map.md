Bank of Namibia data layer map
==============================

Objective
---------

Outline the supervisory, statistical, and operational data flows associated
with the Bank of Namibia, as far as can be understood from public sources.

Suggested views
---------------

- Regulatory reporting from supervised institutions
- Payment system data and monitoring
- Monetary policy and reserves data
- Internal risk, governance, and analytics systems

For each flow or system, capture:

- Data types
- Sources and destinations
- Frequency and timeliness
- Reliance on external infrastructures and standards
