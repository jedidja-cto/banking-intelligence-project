Namclear participant and end-user journeys
==========================================

Objective
---------

Describe journeys that depend on Namclear-operated schemes, from both the
participant and end-user perspectives.

Example journeys
----------------

- A customer making a payment that clears via Namclear
- A bank submitting payment files or transactions to Namclear
- Reconciliation and dispute handling between participants

For each journey, capture:

- Participants and roles
- Steps and timing
- Systems and data exchanged
- Failure modes and risk controls
