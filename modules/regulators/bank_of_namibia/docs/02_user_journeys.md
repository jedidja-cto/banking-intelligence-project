Bank of Namibia stakeholder journeys
====================================

Objective
---------

Describe representative journeys for key stakeholders interacting with the
Bank of Namibia.

Example journeys
----------------

- A commercial bank interacting with supervisory processes
- A payment system seeking approval or oversight
- Government entities interacting on fiscal or debt management matters
- Public communication of policy decisions

For each journey, capture:

- Stakeholders and roles
- Steps and interactions
- Information exchanged
- Systems and channels involved
