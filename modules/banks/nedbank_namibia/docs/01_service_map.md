Deep-dive: Personal > Bank > Accounts > Everyday banking
--------------------------------------------------------

Product
-------

- Silver Pay-as-you-use (N$30 monthly fee)

Positioning
-----------

- Low monthly fee, pay-as-you-use pricing.

Eligibility
-----------

- Namibian resident.
- Age 18+.
- Gross income N$3,000–N$50,000.

KYC documents
-------------

- ID or passport.
- Payslip or proof of income.
- Additional KYC documents as required by the bank.

Capabilities (observed claims)
------------------------------

- Overdraft access (subject to approval).
- Credit card and loans (subject to approval).
- Manage beneficiaries.
- Make transfers.
- Third-party payments (for example DSTV/GoTV).
- Buy electricity.
- Buy airtime.
- Freeze and unfreeze cards.

Cash access
-----------

- ATM.
- In-branch cash services.
- Cash-out at participating supermarkets.

Ways to open
------------

- Web “leave a message” request.
- Facebook IdentityToday selfie sign-up.
- Branch.
- Call centre.
